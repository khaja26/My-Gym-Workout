About the App

Welcome to My Workout Schedule, a user-friendly app designed to help you plan and track your fitness activities efficiently. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, this app is your companion for achieving your fitness goals.

Disclaimer

This app is intended for informational and planning purposes only. It does not replace professional medical or fitness advice. Always consult with a qualified professional before starting any workout or fitness program.

App Guidelines

  • Enter New Data: Click the "Add Data" button to enter new workout details.
  • About the App: Learn more about the features and purpose of the app.
  • Edit Data: Click anywhere in a row of saved data to modify your entries.
  • Share Data: Select a row and click "Share" to send your progress via email or social media.
  • Move Data: When a user selects a row and clicks "Move", the entire entry (except the date) will be copied to a new row. The date of the new row will be updated to reflect 7 days later than the original entry.
  • Timer: Use the timer feature to track your workout duration with dropdown counting.
  • Delete Data: Select a row and click "Delete" to remove unwanted workout data.
  • Track Progress: Monitor your fitness journey and track improvements over time.
  • Manage Subscription: Manage your premium subscription and unlock additional features.
  • Search Videos: Search related videos for workout routines.
